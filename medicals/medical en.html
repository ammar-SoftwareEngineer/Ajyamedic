About Us

Juan Medical Center

It is a center that provides home care services for physiotherapy and has a specialized staff with more than 15 years of experience.

It also provides distinguished competencies to diagnose and examine all cases and develop treatment plans for them to be implemented by physiotherapists and technicians trained by our team.

What sets us apart

Our method is characterized by relying on a comprehensive analysis of the case in all respects, through a clinical examination of the case in the first visit by a physiotherapist.

And taking into account the entire medical history of the case to conclude an accurate diagnosis to ensure the implementation of the optimal treatment plan.

Services

Services provided by the Jaun Medical Center to patients

Physical Rehabilitation For The Elderly.

Rehabilitation of bedridden patients with different medical histories.

Rehabilitation of patients with injuries and neurological diseases.

Post-operative rehabilitation.

Rehabilitation of cerebral palsy cases.
