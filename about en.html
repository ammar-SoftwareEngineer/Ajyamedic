Welcome to Ajyamedic

This company is one of the distinguished companies in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia that includes and is interested in physiotherapy centers. We now have two physiotherapy centers in Saudi Arabia, namely:

- Juan Center for Physiotherapy in Riyadh

- And the Department of Physiotherapy within the Neurology Center

We have a group of highly qualified specialists at the highest level.

Company features

The most important thing that distinguishes us is the speed of dealing with patients and the rapid response to reservations. We also have a staff for home treatment for those who find it difficult to go to the center. Contact us now, and you will feel the difference in a short time, God willing.

Company vision

The company's vision is what is contained in the 2030 plan for the advancement of the Kingdom in the medical field, which is originally based on the idea of operating from abroad in government hospitals, which in turn leads to the advancement of the doctor's efficiency and his provision of high-quality service.
